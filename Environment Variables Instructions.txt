After downloading Android Studio and creating a virtual android device, do this to your PC/LAPTOP:

1. Search and click "Edit environment variables for your account"
2. Under "User variables for My Pc", click "New"
3. Input these:
	3.1 Variable name = ANDROID_HOME
	3.2 Variable value = C:\Users\My Pc\AppData\Local\Android\Sdk
4. Under "System variables", click the "Path" then "Edit"
5. Click "New" and input "C:\Users\My Pc\AppData\Local\Android\Sdk\platform-tools"
6, Then click "Ok" on all new windows.
